My Story

The year was 1985, in Wembley stadium the legendary band Queen performed at the live aid concert. Many years later in 2019 I encountered video sources of this event and the lead singer Freddie Mercury captured my attention. Through my own research I found to my surprise that he was a British Indian, furthermore I then asked myself how many Indian musicians there were in western music. When I struggled to come up with few more than two names I realised I had the opportunity to correct this lack of representation and fulfill my newfound goal of becoming a record producer.
